## Why this fits

Boston metro = Tier 1. AuD is accepted (not PhD). ## Description (from posting)
12-month clinical faculty appointment starting on or after January 1, 2026 in the Department of Communication Sciences and Disorders. Responsibilities: classroom instruction, student supervision in the on-campus clinic (run with Mass Eye and Ear), service to the AuD program, and helping build out a new Audiology Graduate Certificate Program launching June 2026. Doctoral degree in audiology or hearing science from an accredited institution, 5 years post-graduation clinical experience, and MA audiology licensure eligibility.
