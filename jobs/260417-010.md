## Description

NYC DOE audiologists serve deaf/HOH students in District 75 and across the five CSE regions. Duties include assessing need for hearing aids/FM/Infrared assistive listening technology, fitting and verifying devices, attending Annual Reviews/IEPs. CCC-A required (per NY state + DOE). Current-employee per-session opportunities posted internally via InfoHub; external openings periodically posted on DOE careers.
