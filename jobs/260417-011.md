## Description

Pediatric audiologist on a ~45-audiologist team at CHOP. Core duties: audiological assessment, aural rehabilitation, equipment maintenance/calibration, teaching of AuD externs/LEND fellows, and contribution to research protocols. Part of the Department of Audiology, affiliated with Penn's Perelman School of Medicine. Research ecosystem includes the CHOP Research Institute's clinical trial coordinators and the Pediatrics department shared with Penn.
