## Description

The Department of Otolaryngology / Head & Neck Surgery at Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons is hiring an Audiologist at the Assistant Professor level. Responsibilities include complete audiometric evaluations, vestibular testing, electrophysiological testing, and hearing aid evaluation and dispensing at a busy ENT center — primarily based at ColumbiaDoctors Westchester with occasional travel to the NYC practice. Educational duties include teaching medical students and ENT residents plus supervision of audiology student clinicians. Minimum requirements: Doctor of Audiology, New York State Audiologist license, New York Hearing Aid Dispensary license, and active ASHA CCC-A. No firm years-of-experience floor stated in the posting — reasonable entry point for a candidate with 2–3 years post-AuD and research/teaching interest.
