# Pediatric Audiologist — Children's Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP)

## Why it might fit Cece

Tier-2 Philadelphia but CHOP explicitly lists **teaching and research** as part of the pediatric audiologist role — "additional responsibilities including teaching and research, and participation in the ongoing development, revision, and refinement of clinical procedures and protocols." This is exactly the hybrid clinical + research + teaching shape Cece is looking for. CHOP Research Institute has clinical trial coordinators, LEND fellowship program, and strong pediatric hearing-loss research. Non-profit / PSLF eligible. Including despite Tier-2 because the role-shape is distinctive.

## Description

Pediatric audiologist on a ~45-audiologist team at CHOP. Core duties: audiological assessment, aural rehabilitation, equipment maintenance/calibration, teaching of AuD externs/LEND fellows, and contribution to research protocols. Part of the Department of Audiology, affiliated with Penn's Perelman School of Medicine. Research ecosystem includes the CHOP Research Institute's clinical trial coordinators and the Pediatrics department shared with Penn.
