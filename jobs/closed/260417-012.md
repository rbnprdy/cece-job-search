# Clinical Audiologist (adult or pediatric) — Johns Hopkins

## Why it might fit Cece

Tier-2 Baltimore (covered under DC metro), but Johns Hopkins is one of the few AMCs where a clinical audiologist role realistically opens doors to research collaboration — the Division of Audiology sits within the Department of Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery and is research-active. Two open positions (one adult, one pediatric) as of this posting. AuD required; Maryland license required. Non-profit / PSLF eligible. Direct email to cryan1@jhmi.edu rather than ATS.

Division of Audiology in the Johns Hopkins Department of Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery has two clinical audiologist openings starting January 2026 — one primarily pediatric, one primarily adult. Main campus office is at the Johns Hopkins Outpatient Center in Baltimore, with full-time weekly clinic services at six additional locations across Maryland and Washington, DC. Interested candidates should email cover letter + resume/CV to Colleen Ryan (cryan1@jhmi.edu).
